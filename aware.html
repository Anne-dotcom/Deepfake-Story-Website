Counter Measures to spread Awareness



As of now, there are little no help regarding the deepfake issue.



But, there are a few things that can help comabt the deepfake issue.

Legislative Measures



Deepfake’s legal standing is tricky and changing. This harmful content is not outlawed but can breach the law.


The tricky part is that current laws weren’t made with deepfakes in mind. This makes it hard to tackle their unique problems.


Some laws that could help:

    - Privacy Laws
    - Intellectual Property Rights
    - Defamation and Harrassment Laws

Technological Measures



There are many AI and Deepfake detector tools that can help one identify the legitimacy of a video or audio.


The 'Video Authenticator Tool' launched by Microsoft in 2020, detects the amalgamtion boundary of deepfakes.


Recurrent Convolutional Models (RCMs) uses temporal information from images streams across domains and then detects face manipulation in videos. It can detect any type of facial manipulation in a video.
